Things Any Food Business Can Do Today

1
💡 Inventory
Replace your stock spreadsheet with a free Airtable base

Airtable's free tier supports up to 1,000 records. Create a Products table linked to Suppliers, add a formula to flag low stock. Takes about 2 hours and immediately gives you a live, multi-user inventory view. Our PIM guide covers the full setup.

2
2
💡 Automation
Auto-confirm customer orders with a simple Make.com workflow

Make.com (free plan available) watches your inbox and sends a confirmation email automatically when it detects a new order. Saves 5–10 minutes per order and keeps customers informed without anyone on your team lifting a finger.

3
3
💡 eCommerce
Bulk-load your Shopify products using CSV import

Don't enter products one by one. Export your product list from Airtable as CSV, map the columns to Shopify's format, and import in bulk. A 200-product catalogue can be loaded in under an hour with the right template.

4
4
💡 Integration
Check if your system has an API before paying for middleware

Most modern platforms — Airtable, Shopify, Foodit — have free REST APIs. Check the documentation before buying integration tools. Many simple connections can be built directly or with a small Make.com scenario at minimal cost. Ask us if you're unsure.
