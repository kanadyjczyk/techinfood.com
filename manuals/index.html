Manual FAQs

Can I share the manual with my whole team?+
Yes. All manuals are licensed for use within your business. You can share the PDF with your team, print it, and use it as a reference across your organisation. You just can't resell it or distribute it publicly.
What format are the manuals in?+
All manuals are delivered as professionally typeset PDFs. They're designed to be read on screen or printed — whichever works best for your team. They include a table of contents, page numbers, and are formatted for A4 printing.
What if the manual doesn't match my exact setup?+
Our manuals are written to be broadly applicable but focused on the most common setups for food businesses. If something doesn't quite match your situation, every purchase includes 30 days of email support — just ask us and we'll clarify or adapt. For a fully custom version built around your specific systems, use the custom manual request form above.
Can I get a manual customised with my company branding?+
Yes — we offer a branded/customised version of any manual. This adds your logo, colour scheme, and company name throughout, and we can adapt the content to reference your specific tools and processes. Use the custom manual request form for a quote.
Do you offer refunds?+
If you're not satisfied with a manual, contact us within 7 days of purchase and we'll either resolve the issue or issue a full refund — no awkward questions. We want the manuals to be genuinely useful.
